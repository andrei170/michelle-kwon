The opportunity

The ceiling is the business model, not the audience.

Your reach is huge and your content works. The catch: every bit of it points at one offer, 1:1 coaching, sold through a raw Google Form. That is a leak in two ways. You can only coach as many hours as you have, and a bare form is a weak, un-branded way to convert warm leads. The girls who cannot afford 1:1, or are not ready, have nowhere to go.
